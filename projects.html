HOW IT WORKS

Budgeting with stakes.

1
Set your daily budget
Choose a daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly budget. Habitat normalises it to a per-day figure so the math is always simple.
2
Log as you spend
Add expenses with one tap. See your remaining budget shrink in real time. The app nudges you before you overshoot.
3
Protect your streak
Finish the day under budget and your streak grows. Overspend and your streak resets to zero — and your habitat drops a tier.
4
Watch your home evolve
Consistent streaks unlock better habitats, from a cave to a futuristic house. Your home is a live reflection of your discipline.
5
Review your history
See spending patterns by day, week, and month. Understand where your budget goes so you can plan better next time.
6
Stay reminded
Optional daily reminders and streak-at-risk alerts keep you on track. Your data never leaves your phone — fully offline.
